About Me

Kandace Sweat is a Web Application Developer at Daniel Defense, since December 2015. She graduated from the University of Georgia with a Bachelors Degree in Mass Communication and Journalism, and a minor in Communications. Her passion for web and mobile development was born when she decided to get involved with the New Media Institute (NMI) at UGA.

Post-college she worked for LRM a Legal Marketing firm as a Web Designer. Kandace joined Morris Multimedia in July 2015 as the Regional Webmaster. At Morris she managed web design, web advertisement, and web content for at least 10 news organizations in Southeast GA.
